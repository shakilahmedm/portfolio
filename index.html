Areas of Interest

Take a look at some of things I love working on

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. It powers applications like chatbots, sentiment analysis, language translation, and question-answering systems.

Computer Vision

Computer Vision enables machines to interpret and process visual information from the world, such as images and videos. It is widely used in facial recognition, object detection, medical imaging, and autonomous vehicles.

Deep Learning

Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers to learn complex patterns from large datasets. It powers breakthroughs in speech recognition, image classification, and natural language understanding.

Generative AI

Generative AI refers to models that can create new content like text, images, audio, or video, often mimicking human creativity. Technologies like GPT, DALL·E, and Stable Diffusion are examples of generative models used in content creation and augmentation.

Healthcare AI

Healthcare AI leverages machine learning to assist in diagnosing diseases, predicting patient outcomes, and personalizing treatment plans. It plays a crucial role in medical imaging, remote monitoring, and mental health support.

Data Analytics

Data Analytics involves collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to uncover patterns and insights that guide decision-making. It is essential in business, healthcare, finance, and many other domains to drive data-driven strategies.
